Key Features

Kigali Genocide Memorial

A poignant site commemorating the 1994 genocide against the Tutsi in Rwanda, offering educational exhibits and memorial gardens.

Inema Arts Center:

A hub for contemporary Rwandan art, showcasing the creativity and talent of local artists through exhibitions, workshops, and cultural events

Kimironko Market

One of Kigali's largest markets, offering a vibrant atmosphere where you can experience everyday life, taste local foods, and shop for fresh produce, fabrics, and household items.

Nyandungu Eco-Park

ODiscover the lush oasis of Nyandungu Park, an urban sanctuary nestled in the heart of Kigali. With sprawling greenery, serene walking paths, and panoramic views of the city skyline, it offers a peaceful retreat for nature lovers and photographers alike.
